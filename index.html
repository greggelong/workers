Introduction to The Art Workers of the Manual for Everyday Life

The Art Workers of the Manual for Everyday Life is a collective of three artists: David Lane, RedJade Yuan, and Gregory Kreisman. Based in the CaoChangDi art district of Beijing, they explore the intersections of daily life, art, and manual craftsmanship. Through their collaborative efforts, they transform ordinary objects and routines into artistic expressions, inviting viewers to see the beauty and potential in the everyday. Their approach fosters a deeper appreciation of the mundane and celebrates the creativity inherent in manual labor.

《日常生活手册》的艺术民工简介

《日常生活手册》的艺术民工是由三位艺术家组成的团体：David Lane、RedJade Yuan 和 Gregory Kreisman。这个团体位于北京草场地艺术区，通过探索日常生活、艺术和手工艺的交汇点，创作出挑战传统界限的作品。通过他们的合作，他们将普通的物品和日常的活动转化为艺术表达，邀请观众发现日常生活中的美和潜力。他们的创作方法加深了人们对平凡事物的欣赏，庆祝手工劳动中蕴含的创造力。
